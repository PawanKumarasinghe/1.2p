States of Australlia .

New South Wales (NSW)

Sydney, one of the world's most famous cities, the Sydney Opera House, as well as breathtaking coastal vistas can all be found in New South Wales.

Victoria (VIC)

Victoria is well-known for its cultural centre, Melbourne, the picturesque Great Ocean Road, and the jaw-dropping Grampians landscapes.

Queensland (QLD)

Queensland is home to the Great Barrier Reef, the Gold Coast's immaculate beaches, and the Daintree Rainforest, which is known for its lush tropical beauty.

Western Australia (WA)

The expansive Outback, the striking Pinnacles Desert, the breathtaking beaches of Margaret River, and the cosmopolitan city of Perth are all well-known features of Western Australia.

Western Australia (WA)

South Australia is home to the Barossa Valley and McLaren Vale wine regions, the Flinders Ranges' distinctive landscapes, and the charming city of Adelaide.

Tasmania (TAS)

Tasmania is renowned for its unspoiled wilderness, which includes the charming capital city of Hobart, the historic port of Port Arthur, and the Cradle Mountain-Lake St. Clair National Park.
